A little bit about me

I have worked as a tax lawyer for over 12 years before taking a leap of faith and moving to Seattle to earn a law degree with the purpose of joining the academia. Then I gradually realized that I needed to find a career that would nourish my soul by letting me be more artistic and authentic to myself while helping me understand where technology stands as a key to building the future. I was born into a Taiwanese family in São Paulo, Brazil and am currently based in NYC which means I have a big heart split into 3 parts, each belonging to one country, besides having a big appetite as I try to make Brazilian and Taiwanese dishes when I am feeling homesick. My favorite Brazilian snack is pão de queijo, my favorite Taiwanese dish is 粽子, and my favorite American food is...well, I have not decided yet but will update this as soon as I find out!
