A little bit about me

I have worked as a tax lawyer for over 12 years before taking a leap of faith and moving to Seattle to earn a law degree with the purpose of joining the academia. Then I gradually realized that I needed to find a career that would nourish my soul by letting me be more artistic and authentic to myself while helping me understand where technology stands as a key to building the future. Outside of my work, I'm also interested in urban hikes, reading, handcrafting, and languages (the human ones 😁).
